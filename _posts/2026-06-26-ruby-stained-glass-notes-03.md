## I started working on the drawing

My main goal this week was to start working on preparatory drawings.

There's a world between a screenshot and a drawing that works as a potential stained glass piece. And, as I like to say, "building is thinking." I use this a lot when talking about software, but I find it to be true for art too.

So I sat at my living room table and started riffing on the design.

## Square or freeform?

One of the main takeaways from my drawing session was that I realized I like the freeform stained glass panel better than the square one. I find the square-shaped panel to be a tad boring. The freeform, on the other hand: 🤌

I can see it popping up on a desk or in my window.

Here are the two versions of the drawing (with some hand-drawn mock-ups on my phone). Which one do you like best?
